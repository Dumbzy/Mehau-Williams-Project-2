Item Descriptions

Serving both an educative and comedic purpose, the Item Description feature provides exactly that - a description about any given item listing information and stats that pertain to it. Most items, however mundane they may or may not be will also have backstories (not to be interpreted as historical fact), with these item descriptions being similar to what can be found in Dark Souls. Given the aesthetic of ALTAR the manner of these descriptions will be more grounded towards sci-fi related themes.
