ALTAR

An AR RPG experience with you as the protagonist and your life as the narrative.

ALTAR is an AR application meant to liven up as well as recontextualize the users environment; providing a fun yet helpful experience, catering towards an audience who are fans of the RPG genre.

Player Statistics

ALTAR keeps track of both the good and bad qualities that comprise your character meaning, who you are as a person.

Item Descriptions

An insightful feature which uses Image Recognition Technology to provide descriptions of objects in your vicinity.

Reward System

Taking advantage of the Daily Missions feature, users will be able to accumulate points based on completed tasks.
