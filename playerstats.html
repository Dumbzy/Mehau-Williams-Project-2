Player Statistics

ALTAR gives users the ability to get a grand overview of their own traits; whether its their own competency of completing certain tasks, likes and dislikes, strengths and weaknesses, ailments along with many basic discriptors of the user. The use of this AR application in regards to this feature aims to serve as a method of monitoring ones self. In conjunction with the Daily Missions feature which is housed within the Reward System, the user can customize a routine to achieve certain milestones to increase ones own player stats.

Star Wars Knights of the Old Republic Character Customization is one of the precedents for this feature.
