About

~As a preface this website serves as concept for a potential AR application and features discussed may or may not appear in the final build~

The purpose of ALTAR is to change up ones lifestyle either for leisurely value or self improvement. Gamers will resonate well with the features of ALTAR although individuals who wish to be more adventurous and direct their own narrative will be drawn to this application also. We want users to gain control over their own goals and feel good about achieving milestones they have set out for themselves in a friendly and Sci-Fi inspired environment.
